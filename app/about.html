My Story

I discovered my passion for design during my early years in Yogyakarta — a city that breathes art and culture. Surrounded by traditional Javanese aesthetics and a thriving creative community, I developed an eye for detail and a deep appreciation for visual storytelling.

Currently pursuing my degree in Visual Communication Design, I've honed my skills across branding, packaging, editorial design, and digital media. I believe great design is the intersection of beauty and function — where every element serves a purpose.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring local art exhibitions, experimenting with new design tools, or finding inspiration in Yogyakarta's vibrant street art scene. Every experience fuels my creative vision.
